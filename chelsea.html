The Construction of Chelsea Piers

Early Era at the Piers

The origin of Chelsea Piers begain in the early 21st century as a mid-tier resort on the lower east side of Manhattan. It originally only included pier 60.

After decades of success, Chelsea Piers eventually acquired pier 61, also known as "The lighthouse". This event venue was mainly used for weddings at the time, though not it used for a multitude of mid-sized gatherings.

1990's Era and Addition of pier 59

The '90s saw the expansion of Chelea Piers further and the need for an even more intimate venue arose. This would be used for small parties, things such as:

  • Bar/Bat mitzvahs
  • Birthday parties
  • Showcases
  • Small Weddings
